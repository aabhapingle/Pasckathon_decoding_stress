Stress Free

Companion Survey Test



1. In the last month, how often has the patient been upset because
of something that happened unexpectedly?






2. In the last month, how often has the patient felt that he/she
was unable to control the important things in his/her life?






3. In the last month, how often has the patient felt
nervous and “stressed”?






4. In the last month, how often has the patient felt
confident about his/her ability to handle his/her personal problems?






5. In the last month, how often has the patient felt that
things were going his/her way?






6. In the last month, how often did the patient feel that he/she
could not cope with all the things that he/she had to do?






7. In the last month, how often has the patient been able to
control irritations in his/her life?






8. In the last month, how often did the patient feel
that he/she was on top of things?






9. In the last month, how often has the patient angered because
of things that were outside of his/her control?






10. In the last month, how often has the patient felt difficulties
were piling up so high that he/she could not overcome them?
